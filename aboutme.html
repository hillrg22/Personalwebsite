About Me

I'm a current student at Galvanize Inc. in Denver, Colorado. I have 5+ years of experience in sales and management, and I decided recently that it was about time to explore a field that has always intrigued me!

My wife and I moved to Colorado in 2015, and we absolutely love it! We are both from Minnesota originally, though we met while we were living in Arizona.

My favorite thing about Colorado is the breadth of outdoor activities available in both the Summer and Winter. I love to ski and go adventuring with our dog, Monte.

I've lived all over the country, and exploring both the US and other countries is a passion of mine.

One of my favorite recent trips took me to South Africa, and my first time visiting the African continent did not disappoint. I've got a major urge to go back, but there are too many other places that I've got to check off my list first.
